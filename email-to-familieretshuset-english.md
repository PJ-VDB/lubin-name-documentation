**We are submitting herewith a signed declaration from [Name], Mayor of [Town], confirming that Lubin is a first name in use in France (Appendix 1).**

The declaration is based on INSEE's publicly published data — the official French state authority for civil registration statistics — and confirms that 3,935 children have been registered with the first name Lubin in France since 2000, of whom 2,530 were registered since 2010. At its peak in 2012, Lubin ranked 282nd out of 6,823 boys' names given in France that year.

We note that the Danish Embassy in France, in their response, itself cited the INSEE interactive tool as a reference — the very same official source on which the mayor's declaration is based. By Denmark's own domestic standard, a name is considered "in use" with as few as 25 living bearers of the same sex. France has approximately 11 times Denmark's population. Even adjusted for population, the 2,530 boys named Lubin in France since 2010 — peaking at 245 in a single year — vastly exceeds what Denmark considers sufficient domestically.
